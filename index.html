About

I am an Economist at the Bank of Korea. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from The Ohio State University.

My research focuses on household finance, labor economics, and family economics. I study how households respond to income shocks, credit constraints, and housing policies, with particular emphasis on debt repayment, financial distress, and household formation.
